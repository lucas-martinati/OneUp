Privacy Policy

Règles de Confidentialité

Dernière mise à jour : 13 mars 2026

OneUp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by OneUp.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

We use Google Sign-In to authenticate users. When you use this feature, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your basic Google profile information (display name, profile picture)

b. Health & Fitness Data

To provide the core functionality of OneUp, we collect and store health and fitness data that you enter into the app, including but not limited to:

  • Exercise data (e.g., number of push-ups, workout sessions)
  • Habit tracking data (e.g., daily habits, streaks, progress)
  • Any other fitness or wellness metrics you choose to log

This data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore and is used solely to provide and improve your personal experience within the app. We do not sell, share, or transfer your health data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

c. Usage Data

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app. This includes:

  • App launches and session duration
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and performance data

This data is collected in an anonymized form and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our application.
  • Authenticate your user account.
  • Store and display your personal fitness and habit progress.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our application.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore, a cloud database provided by Google (Firebase). Your account and progress data (habits, exercise logs, etc.) is retained for as long as your account remains active.

If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days of account deletion.

Anonymous analytics data collected via Firebase Analytics may be retained for up to 14 months, in accordance with Google's default retention settings.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time. You can do so by:

1. In-app deletion: Go to Settings → Données & Cloud → Supprimer mon compte within the OneUp app to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

2. By email: Contact us at the email address below with a deletion request, and we will process it within 30 days.

Upon deletion, all personally identifiable data (account info, health data, habit data) will be permanently removed from our databases.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with:

  • Firebase / Google: For secure data storage, authentication, and analytics (see Google's Privacy Policy).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (see Section 4).
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.
